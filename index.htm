Questions

  • Q: What is PEPE MATRIX?

    A: PEPE MATRIX is a blockchain platform that aims to develop an eco-friendly blockchain technology while maintaining its security and decentralization. It is built on the Binance Smart Chain and features a decentralized token, a DEX, staking, and a launchpad.

  • Q: What is staking on PEPE MATRIX?

    A: Staking on PEPE MATRIX involves holding a certain amount of tokens to secure the network and earn rewards. Users who stake their tokens help to validate transactions on the blockchain and are rewarded with more tokens for their efforts.

  • Q: What is the PEPE MATRIX launchpad?

    A: The PEPE MATRIX launchpad is a platform for hosting and launching new projects on the PEPE MATRIX blockchain. It allows for easy fundraising, community participation, and project incubation.
